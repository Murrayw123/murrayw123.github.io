About

Graduating in 2014 with a Bachelor of Arts (Music) and Diploma of Education I worked across all years as a music teacher for primary and highschool. During this time I developed an interest in programming, stemming from a long held hobby, building computers and tinkering around with operating systems (Richard Stallman is a personal hero). I completed online courses in programming and computer science and decided to take the hobby further, completing university units Introduction to Operating Systems and Python, Object Oriented Programming in Java, Precalculus and Discrete Mathematics. Since then I've been working at the Department of Education as a highschool Computer Science teacher, developing a real interest in educational software, instructional design and online e-learning opportunities within the realm of databases and programming.

In the future I would love to combine my interests in software development and education. What form this will take I'm not yet sure but if you have any ideas please get in touch :)

In my spare time (when I'm away from a computer) I love all things outdoors, and in particular cycling. I can often be found out on the Munda Biddi, Western Australia's premier offroad cycling trail. Alternatively I'll be planning my next trip to Tasmania, a place I fell in love with last year after completing the Overland Track (pictured.)
