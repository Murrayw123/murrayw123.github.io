Teaching

Working as a Computing/Computer Science teacher for the Department of Education WA, I'm currently focused on implementing the new Digital Technologies curriculum for years 7-10, developing teaching materials for ATAR Computer Science and figuring out new and exciting ways to integrate ICT into the classroom.

Below you'll find some links to the forward planning and teaching plans I've developed. E-learning

Through my time teaching ATAR Computer Science, I have developed an online databases course/tutorial series that maps to the SCSA Computer Science Syllabus for year 12. SQLite has been used due to the fact it's trivial to run on both Windows and OSX. For those teachers that may wish to use this, or use some of the materials please get in touch.
